I'm about to learn you a thing or two about neurolinguistic programming, buckle your seatbelts.

  • Neuro-linguistic programming (NLP) is an approach to communication, personal development, and psychotherapy.
  • It was created by Richard Bandler and John Grinder in California, United States in the 1970s.
  • I first learned about it from the book "Unleash the Power Within" by Tony Robbins.
  • It's super neato and can be used to rewire your brain towards success and fulfillment.
